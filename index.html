Jinlong Peng (彭瑾龙)

Senior Researcher
Google Scholar / GitHub

I obtained the master's degree at 2019 and bachelor's degree at 2016 both from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, advised by Prof. Weiyao Lin.

I am currently a Senior Researcher at Tencent Youtu Lab. My research interests are in AIGC, image editing, multiple object tracking, detection, segmentation, anomaly detection.

I am looking for highly motivated interns working on exciting computer vision projects based in Shanghai. If you are interested, please send an email to my email jeromepeng@tencent.com.
